Creating Assets:
1 - Save transparent image
2 - Open in gimp
3 - Crop canvas to image (as square as you can get)
4 - Save as .xcf
5 - Image > Canvas Size, make 300x300
6 - Tools > Transform Tools > Scale, make 300x300
7 - Center Image on Canvas, adjust to liking
8 - Export as "<asset_name>_big.png", no compression
9 - Repeat 5-7 but with size 128x128
10 - Export as "<asset_name>_small.png", no compression
11 - For convenience, don't save changes to .xcf file
12 - Copy small and big .png to grit /assets folder
